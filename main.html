Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto, the cultural heart of Japan, is known for its stunning temples, beautiful gardens, and traditional tea houses.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Fushimi Inari Taisha – Walk through thousands of vibrant torii gates leading to the sacred shrine.
  2. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) – A breathtaking Zen temple covered in gold leaf, reflecting beautifully on the pond.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Forest – Experience the mystical whispers of the tall bamboo groves.
  4. Kiyomizu-dera – A UNESCO heritage temple offering a stunning panoramic view of Kyoto.
  5. Gion District – The best place to spot a geisha and enjoy Kyoto’s traditional atmosphere.

Must-Try Foods

  • Kaiseki – A traditional multi-course Japanese meal with seasonal ingredients.
  • Yudofu – Tofu hot pot cooked in a light broth, famous in Kyoto's temples.
  • Matcha – Enjoy a cup of rich, ceremonial-grade green tea in a traditional tea house.
  • Yatsuhashi – A famous Kyoto sweet made of cinnamon-flavored rice flour.
  • Saba Sushi – Kyoto-style sushi with marinated mackerel, wrapped in bamboo leaves.
